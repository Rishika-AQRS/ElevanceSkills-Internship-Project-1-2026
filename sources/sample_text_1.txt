A chatbot is a software application designed to simulate human conversation.
It can interact with users through text or voice, answering questions, providing explanations, and guiding users through tasks.
Modern chatbots often use large language models (LLMs) and retrieval‑augmented generation (RAG) to provide accurate, up‑to‑date answers.
Chatbots are widely used in customer support, education, healthcare, and e‑commerce.
